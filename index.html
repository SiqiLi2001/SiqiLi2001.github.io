About Me

I am currently a Research Associate in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, supervised by Prof. Tay Wee Peng.

I received my B.Eng. in Automation from Ocean University of China (2019-2023, Qingdao). Between 2021 and 2023, I was supervised by Prof. Yao Peng on research in path planning and multi-agent systems.

From 2024 to 2025, I completed my M.Sc. in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, supervised by Prof. Tay Wee Peng and closely guided by Dr. Wang. My research during this period focused on Computer Vision, particularly 3D vision and localization.
