Selected Publications

Trustworthy Human-in-the-Loop Routing for Multimodal Transit

Siqi Xin, A. Tan, L. Zhang. IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems, 2024.

We introduce a calibrated decision-support framework that integrates operator feedback with spatiotemporal passenger flow predictions to improve routing reliability.

Data-Driven Resilience Planning for Urban Infrastructure

Siqi Xin, M. Rahman, C. Y. Cho. In Proceedings of the AAAI Workshop on AI for Social Impact, 2023.

Developed adaptive models that couple risk-aware reinforcement learning with municipal asset data to prioritize infrastructure maintenance.

Interactive Dashboards for Community Mobility Insights

J. Lim, Siqi Xin, P. Ong. ACM CSCW Companion, 2022.

Presented a visual analytics platform that augments crowd-sourced mobility data with predictive analytics to inform community-led interventions.
